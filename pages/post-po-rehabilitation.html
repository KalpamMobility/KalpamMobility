Post P&O Rehabilitation

Comprehensive therapy to maximize function with your orthotic or prosthetic device

Optimizing Your Mobility

Our specialized rehabilitation program helps patients adapt to and maximize the benefits of their orthotic or prosthetic devices. Through personalized therapy, we help you regain strength, mobility, and confidence in daily activities.

Key Benefits:

  • Faster adaptation to new devices
  • Improved strength and endurance
  • Proper gait training and movement patterns
  • Pain management strategies
  • Increased independence in daily activities

Our Rehabilitation Focus

Gait Training

Developing proper walking mechanics

Strength Training

Building muscle for better device control

Balance Therapy

Improving stability and coordination

ADL Training

Mastering daily activities with your device
