Gait Training

Specialized movement therapy to improve walking patterns and mobility

Restoring Natural Movement

Our gait training program helps patients regain proper walking mechanics after injury, surgery, or neurological conditions. Using evidence-based techniques and advanced equipment, we help you achieve safer, more efficient movement.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves balance and coordination
  • Reduces risk of falls
  • Corrects abnormal walking patterns
  • Increases walking speed and endurance
  • Enhances overall mobility

Conditions We Address

Neurological Conditions

Stroke, MS, Parkinson's, and CP

Orthopedic Injuries

Post-fracture or joint replacement

Prosthetic Training

Adapting to new artificial limbs

Mobility Transitions

From wheelchair to walking
