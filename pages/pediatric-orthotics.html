Pediatric Orthotics

Specialized supports designed for growing children's unique needs

Supporting Healthy Development

Our pediatric orthotic solutions are specially designed to accommodate children's growing bodies while correcting alignment, improving mobility, and promoting natural development. From infants to teenagers, we create comfortable devices that children will actually wear.

Key Benefits:

  • Lightweight designs for active children
  • Adjustable for growth spurts
  • Fun colors and patterns kids love
  • Corrects developmental abnormalities
  • Improves mobility and independence

Conditions We Address

Cerebral Palsy

Supports to improve gait and positioning

Clubfoot

Bracing for Ponseti method treatment

Spina Bifida

Custom supports for mobility

Toe Walking

Bracing to encourage heel strike
