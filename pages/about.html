About Kalpam Clinic

Expert prosthetic and orthotic care in Amravati, restoring mobility and independence

Our Practice

Kalpam Clinic brings specialized prosthetic and orthotic solutions to Amravati, combining technical expertise with compassionate patient care. Founded by Vaibhav Thakre, our clinic is dedicated to improving quality of life through customized mobility solutions.

We serve patients of all ages, from children needing their first orthotic devices to adults requiring advanced prosthetic limbs. Our personalized approach ensures each patient receives the attention and care they deserve.

Our Expert Practitioner

With extensive training and hands-on experience across premier healthcare institutions, our lead practitioner brings exceptional expertise to every patient case. We combine technical precision with compassionate care to deliver optimal mobility solutions.
