Pressure Garments

Custom therapeutic garments for scar management and edema control

Specialized Compression Therapy

Our custom pressure garments provide medically prescribed compression to manage scars, control swelling, and support healing. Designed for comfort and effectiveness, these garments help improve functional and cosmetic outcomes after burns, surgery, or trauma.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduces hypertrophic and keloid scarring
  • Controls edema and lymphedema
  • Improves circulation and healing
  • Custom-fit for optimal pressure
  • Breathable, skin-friendly fabrics

Garment Types

Burn Recovery

Full-body suits and partial garments for burn scars

Compression garments after cosmetic/reconstructive surgery

Gradient compression for swelling management
