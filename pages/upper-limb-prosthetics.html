Upper Limb Prosthetics

Advanced arm and hand prostheses restoring function and confidence

Regaining Dexterity and Function

Our custom upper limb prosthetics combine advanced engineering with personalized fitting to restore meaningful function. From basic body-powered devices to sophisticated myoelectric systems, we provide solutions tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Key Benefits:

  • Restored grip and manipulation abilities
  • Natural-looking cosmetic options
  • Myoelectric control for intuitive movement
  • Lightweight designs for all-day comfort
  • Activity-specific terminal devices

Prosthesis Types

Transradial

Below-elbow prostheses with various terminal devices

Transhumeral

Above-elbow prostheses with elbow mechanisms

Myoelectric

Electrically powered with muscle signal control

Activity-Specific

Specialized devices for work, sports, and hobbies
